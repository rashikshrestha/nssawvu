About Us

The Nepalese Students and Scholars Association (NSSA) at West Virginia University is a student-led organization that brings together Nepali students, scholars, and friends. We aim to promote Nepali culture, support academic and professional growth, and foster a sense of community among members.

Our activities include cultural events, social gatherings, academic workshops, and community service. We welcome everyone interested in Nepali culture and values.
