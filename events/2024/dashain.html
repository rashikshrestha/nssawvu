Dashain Cultural Program 2024

🎉 We're excited to announce that our Dashain celebration is scheduled for Sunday, October 13th, at Chestnut Ridge Park- Dining Hall! 🏞️

This year, we have planned an exciting lineup of cultural events such as:
🕉️ Tika program   🎶 Dances & Songs   🎭 Acting performances   🎲 Games

We encourage you to participate and help make this event memorable by showcasing your talents. Please fill out the attached sign-in sheet to indicate your participation and help us plan accordingly. 📝 Knowing the headcount will assist us in making appropriate food arrangements.

As all of you know, as part of our tradition, NSSA collects an annual membership fee from each adult. 🙏
Please kindly send your membership fee to our treasurer, Ankit Paudel. The fee is $15 per adult in your household, so for families with two adults, please send $30, and adjust accordingly based on the number of adults.

Please make sure to include your name in the transfer notes to ensure accurate record-keeping. If you wish to contribute beyond the standard fee, your additional support will be greatly appreciated and will help enrich our celebrations of Dashain and the upcoming events. 🎁

More information about the exact time and further details of the celebration will be provided closer to the event date. Please stay tuned. 📢

If you have any questions or need more information, please don’t hesitate to reach out. 📞

Looking forward to seeing all your performances and celebrating together! 🥳
