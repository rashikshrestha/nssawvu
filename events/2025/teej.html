Teej Cultural Program 2025

We're excited to announce our Teej Cultural Program 2025!

  • 📅 Date: Saturday, August 23, 2025
  • 🕒 Time: 1:00 PM – 6:00 PM
  • 📍 Location: WVU Mountainlair – Small Blue Ballroom (236SB)

Teej is a vibrant festival celebrated by Nepali women with music, dance, and traditional rituals. Our Teej Cultural Program features group dances, delicious food, and a joyful gathering to honor Nepali heritage and promote community bonding.

This will be a vibrant celebration of Nepalese culture, with:

  • Cultural dances & traditional Teej songs 💃
  • Karaoke & performances 🎤
  • Potluck dinner 🍲
  • Photography session 📸

We will be sharing a meal together potluck style.

Dress Suggestion:

Women are encouraged to wear a traditional Sari to embrace the Teej spirit, but this is completely optional. Feel free to wear whatever you’re comfortable in.
Men are welcome to wear casual attire or traditional Nepali clothing if they’d like, but there’s no strict dress code.

Let’s make this a colorful and memorable celebration together!
