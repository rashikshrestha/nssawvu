Camping 2025

Camping season is here and we're excited to announce that our NSSA camping trip is officially set for August 8–10, 2025! This time, we’ll be heading to Prince Gallitzin State Park in Pennsylvania — about 2 hours and 30 minutes from Morgantown. We’ve reserved a rustic group tent site (Site 007), so get ready for a weekend full of fresh air, campfires, and summer vibes!

More details about rides, food, and activities will be shared as the date gets closer — but for now, save the date, and let us know if you’re in!

Below are the essential trip details to keep you on track:
  • 📍 Campsite Details
  • Site Name: 007 – Group Tent Camping Area
  • Location: Prince Gallitzin State Park, Pennsylvania
  • Coordinates: 40.66932, -78.54128
  • Check-in: 3:00 PM, Friday, August 8
  • Check-out: 3:00 PM, Sunday, August 10
  • Site Type: Rustic Organized Group Tenting Site
🌡️ Important Notes
  • It will get chilly at night! The temperature is expected to drop to 58°F, so please pack warm clothing and blankets to stay comfortable.
  • 🧊 Everyone MUST bring one full case of drinking water in their car. We will be in a rustic area, so this is mandatory for safety and convenience.
🎒 Packing Checklist
Essentials (must bring):
  • Tent
  • Sleeping pad / Air mattress
  • Blankets
  • Pillow
  • Slippers
  • Headlamp or flashlight
  • Insect repellent
  • Camp chairs
  • 🚰 One full case of water
  • 🧻 Toilet paper – even if pit toilets are stocked, backup is smart
  • 🧼 Hand sanitizer / Wet wipes – for hygiene at the campsite
Optional (nice to have):
  • Swimwear (there’s a lake and beach nearby)
  • Board games
  • ⚽ Outdoor games (football, volleyball, frisbee, etc.)
⚠️ VERY IMPORTANT: NO ALCOHOL POLICY

Please remember that alcohol is strictly prohibited in all Pennsylvania state parks, including Prince Gallitzin.
Violations can lead to fines or removal from the park. Let’s all be responsible and make sure we follow the rules.
