Dashain Celebration 2024

Dashain is the biggest festival for Nepali people. The NSSA organized a vibrant Dashain celebration at WVU, featuring traditional food, tika, music, and cultural performances. The event brought together students, scholars, and families to celebrate and share Nepali culture.

Welcome Program 2024

The Welcome Program is an annual event to greet new students at WVU. The program includes fun games, food, and an introduction to the Nepali community and resources at WVU. It helps new students feel at home and make new friends.

Sports Day 2024

Sports Day is a highlight for NSSA members, featuring soccer, volleyball, and other games. The event encourages teamwork, fitness, and fun for all ages. Prizes and refreshments are provided for participants and spectators.
